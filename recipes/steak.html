Steak Recipe

How to make the perfect reverse seared steak

This recipe was developed over the years, I followed numerous guides on cooking to end up at this recipe.

Ingredients

  • Steak: Ribeye or NY Strip, 1.5 - 2.5 inches thick
  • Smoker/Oven
  • Skillet
  • Meat Thermometer
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Cooking Oil: Tallow or Butter preferred but your choice

Steps

  1. Salt steak 1-2 hours prior to cooking
  2. Preheat Smoker/Oven to 250f
  3. Place steak in smoker/oven
  4. Check temp periodically until 10-15 degrees under your preferred doneness
  5. Preheat you skillet as high as possible
  6. Add cooking oil to the skllet
  7. Add steak to skillet
  8. Sear for 30-60 seconds per side or until your preference and then flip and repeat
  9. Season steak with Black Pepper and let sit for 5-10 minutes and eat.
